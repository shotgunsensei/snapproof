Build a polished, production-grade SaaS web application called **SnapProof AI** for **Shotgun Ninjas Productions**.

## PRODUCT SUMMARY
SnapProof AI is a mobile-first field documentation and proof-of-work platform for MSP technicians, field IT engineers, mechanics, contractors, inspectors, and service operators.

The app turns:
- photos
- voice notes
- typed notes
- findings
- parts used
- labor details

into:
- client-ready reports
- proof-of-work summaries
- estimate drafts
- change-order drafts
- executive summaries
- branded PDF closeout documents
- shareable customer report links

This is not a generic note-taking app, not a PSA, and not a bloated CRM. It is a sharp, focused “capture to proof to payment” workflow tool.

The experience must feel fast, premium, modern, and immediately useful.

---

## PRIMARY BUSINESS GOAL
Create a SaaS product that is:
1. easy to understand in under 15 seconds
2. visually impressive enough to create trust
3. operationally simple enough for small businesses and solo users
4. valuable enough that users want to pay quickly
5. extensible into future white-label MSP and field service versions
